Solvemate is a web-based tool designed to simplify and visualize key numerical methods such as:

  • Taylor Series (1 & 2 variables)
  • Newton-Raphson Method
  • Euler & Modified Euler Methods
  • Runge-Kutta Method (RK4)

It helps students understand step-by-step iterations, view results as tables, and even visualize functions through interactive graphs.
