About Us
Solvemate is a student-built web application that helps solve and visualize numerical methods with accuracy and ease. It supports core methods like Taylor Series, Euler's Method, Modified Euler, Runge-Kutta (RK4), and Newton-Raphson. Designed for engineering and math students, Solvemate breaks down complex computations into clear, step-by-step results — with interactive graphs and clean tables for better understanding.
